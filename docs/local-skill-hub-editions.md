# Local Skill Hub Editions

| Edition | Registration | Scope | Included |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Community Core | No | One machine | Local CLI/search/serve, local indexing, local learning logs, installers, and public self-update. |
| Registered Local Skill Hub | Yes | One hub, up to 100 active client instances | Public, registered, community, and Team Free collections when allowlisted; allowlist-only distribution. |
| Business | Yes, paid | More than 100 active client instances, multiple hubs | Private team packs, dashboard, support, larger limits, and multiple hub coordination. |
| Enterprise | Contracted | Private registry and governed rollout | Enterprise Skill Lock, signed policies, SSO/on-prem/VPC options, private registry, and business governance controls. |

The Registered Local Skill Hub free tier covers up to 100 active client instances. An active client is a registered agent/runtime client that contacted the hub in the last 30 days.

Full catalog distribution is disabled while the private audit verdict is `YES_WITH_ALLOWLIST`.
