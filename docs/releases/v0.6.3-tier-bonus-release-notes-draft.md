## Headline

**v0.6.3 — Learning Loop that actually improves your skills, locally.** Record
when a skill was missed, wrong, or unhelpful; get safe, local improvement
candidates; preview every change as a non-mutating dry-run. Nothing is uploaded,
nothing is auto-applied.

## What's new (all tiers)

- **Missed / wrong-skill feedback** — feedback is no longer just accept/reject;
  you can record that the router *missed* a skill or picked the *wrong* one.
- **Local improvement candidates** — turn feedback into a reviewable list of
  proposed improvements (ranking hints, doc fixes, draft skills).
- **Dry-run previews** — see exactly what a candidate would change, with nothing
  written to your library.
- **Learning doctor** — a one-command health check for the local loop.

## Per-tier bonus

- **Free / community-core:** the full local loop above — no registration, no
  telemetry, no data leaving your machine.
- **Registered:** a **registered-ready candidate report** — a sanitized,
  schema-versioned local artifact so your improvements stay forward-compatible
  with future catalog/update workflows. Stays local; no upload in v0.6.3.
- **Team:** a **redacted team review packet** and a **manual approval flow** —
  review candidates together, approve/reject/defer by hand, keep a local audit
  trail. No dashboard, no private publishing, no SLA.
- **Business:** a **local Learning Backlog Export** — improvement candidates rolled
  up into a prioritized maintenance queue (severity, affected skill/workflow class,
  review status). Dashboard-ready for the future; no live dashboard or hosted audit
  log today.
- **Enterprise:** an **audit-safe Learning Evidence Pack** and a **No-Auto-Apply
  Governance Contract** — diagnostics and previews only, respecting Enterprise
  Skill Lock and approved-source boundaries, with redacted local audit evidence.

## Privacy & safety (applies to every tier)

- Local-first: no telemetry, no auto-upload, no hosted calls by default.
- Stored data is counts, buckets, verdicts, skill names, and action classes —
  never raw prompts, task text, queries, secrets, tokens, keys, skill bodies, MCP
  schemas, or absolute local paths.
- Dry-run is non-mutating. No candidate is ever applied or published automatically.
- Archive integrity is **SHA256-verified**; we do not claim cryptographic
  signatures unless signing is actually in use.

## Explicitly NOT in v0.6.3

No hosted submission/sync, no live dashboards, no hosted audit logs, no SSO/SCIM,
no license server, no billing/entitlement enforcement, no marketplace feedback, no
remote learning, no auto-apply, no full-catalog distribution.
